HOW TO PLAY

GOAL - Put your organizational skills and endurance to the test by clearing as many lines as possible.
CLEAR LINES - Maneuver the falling Bricks to fit them together within the Matrix. To clear a line fill every square within a single row.
SCORE POINTS - Earn points by clearing lines. Clear multiple lines at once to increase your scoring opportunities.
NEXT QUEUE - Preview the upcoming Brick in the Next Queue to plan ahead and increase your scoring opportunities.
GAME OVER - Stack the Bricks too high and the game is over!
